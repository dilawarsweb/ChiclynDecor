About Chiclyn Decor

Discover our Electric Fusion philosophy and the story behind America's premier luxury home décor consultancy.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Chiclyn Decor emerged from a simple yet powerful idea: luxury home design should be as dynamic as the people who live in these spaces. Our founder, Lyla Chiclyn, observed that traditional luxury interiors often felt static and impersonal—beautiful but lacking energy.

Drawing inspiration from her background in contemporary art and her travels across vibrant global cities, Lyla developed the Electric Fusion philosophy. This approach combines the timeless elegance of luxury design with bold, energetic elements that reflect modern lifestyles.

The Electric Fusion Philosophy

At the heart of Chiclyn Decor is our signature Electric Fusion philosophy—a design approach that harmonizes contrasting elements to create spaces that are both sophisticated and vibrant.

Core Principles:

  • Dynamic Contrast: We intentionally juxtapose elements—soft and hard, warm and cool, traditional and avant-garde—to create visual interest and energy.
  • Personalized Energy: Every space should reflect the energy and personality of its inhabitants, not follow rigid design rules.
  • Layered Luxury: True luxury comes from thoughtful layering of textures, materials, and lighting that evolves with time and use.
  • Sustainable Elegance: We source premium materials with consideration for environmental impact and longevity.

Our Team

Our team of design consultants brings together diverse expertise in interior design, architecture, art history, and sustainable design. Each team member undergoes rigorous training in the Electric Fusion methodology and participates in continuous education to stay at the forefront of design innovation.

Our Process

  1. Discovery Consultation: We begin with an in-depth conversation to understand your vision, lifestyle, and aspirations.
  2. Energy Assessment: Our unique approach includes assessing the natural energy flow of your space and how you interact with it.
  3. Concept Development: We create tailored design concepts that blend your preferences with Electric Fusion principles.
  4. Collaborative Refinement: We work closely with you to refine the design until it perfectly captures your vision.
  5. Precise Execution: Our project management team oversees every detail of implementation, from sourcing to installation.
  6. Final Energizing: We complete every project with thoughtful styling that brings the space to life.

Service Offerings

At Chiclyn Decor, we offer a comprehensive suite of luxury home décor services designed to meet every need, from complete home transformations to focused design consultations. Each service incorporates our signature Electric Fusion philosophy.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and understanding how you use our site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization.
  • Targeting Cookies: These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Session Cookies: These are temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. We use these to maintain your session and remember your preferences during your visit.

Persistent Cookies: These cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. We use these to recognize you when you return to our website.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may also use cookies from third-party partners for analytics and advertising purposes. These partners may use cookies to collect information about your activities across different websites.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit our site and some services and functionalities may not work.

6. Your Choices Regarding Cookies

Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings. To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.
